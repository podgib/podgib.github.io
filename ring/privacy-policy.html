Ring for Kerv

Privacy policy

The app collects and transmits Kerv account information to the Kerv website for the purpose of signing into your Kerv account. The account information is stored only the device for the purpose of maintaining your session. Your Kerv balance and transaction details are obtained directly from Kerv, and never transmitted beyond your device.

The app does not collect, store or transmit any personally identifable information.

The app collects and transmits anonymous usage data, device metadata and error logs to Microsoft Azure Mobile Center for the purpose of analytics and improving the service.

This policy may change from time to time and is available on this website.
